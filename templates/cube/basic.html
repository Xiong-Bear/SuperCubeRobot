Use the mouse to turn the cube.

Turn the faces with the U/D/L/R/B/F/u/d/l/r/b/f/M/E/S/x/y/z keys.

Hold shift to turn faces couter-clockwise. Note: Operations are case sensitive.

Press scramble to randomly scramble the cube. Press solve to solve the cube using deep learning!
